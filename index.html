Welcome to the official website of FRC Bot!

FRC bot is a FIRST Robotics Data Chatbot for Facebook Messenger.

You can ask the bot to give you informations about

A team webiste

The location of a team

The nickname of a team

The next match time for a team

The stats of a team in their current event
